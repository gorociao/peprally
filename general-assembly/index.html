General Assembly: Video Branding

General Assembly is dedicated to rethinking education in the tech sector. Their business model includes a co-working space, in addition to creating a library of on-line classes to help students learn how to start companies. We brought their miniature-model branding to life and created a new kind of online broadcast package that gave a nod to the entirety of what the company is about.
