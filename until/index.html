Until

Until asks: What you are you looking forward to? The minimalist app's main virtue is that is runs in notifications center, so it’s easy to countdown along side daily alerts. We used the project as an excuse to experiment with Swift, Apple’s new programming language and Facebook Pop, an animation framework.
